Cryptique Secures Strategic Angel Investment to Build for the future of the Internet

P
Parth Agarwal
May 15, 2025
3 min read

Cryptique is proud to announce the successful close of its angel investment round, marking a significant milestone in our mission to build the foundational infrastructure for Web3 marketing.

Building for Tomorrow's Internet

We are not building for today's internet where Web3 companies are vastly outnumbered, but for a future where decentralized businesses become the norm. As the ecosystem matures, the need for robust, transparent, and privacy-centric marketing analytics becomes critical. Cryptique addresses this by enabling accurate attribution in a space where traditional Web2 tools fall short.

"We are not building for today's internet where Web3 companies are vastly outnumbered, but for a future where decentralized businesses become the norm."

Strategic Partnership Beyond Capital

This round was about more than capital. We strategically brought on board leading operators, builders, and investors from the Web3 ecosystem who share our vision and bring deep domain expertise. Their insight and networks will be instrumental in shaping our trajectory as we scale.

Accelerating Platform Development

With this backing, we are accelerating the development of our platform delivering intelligence, attribution, and growth tools tailored for the new internet.

What This Means for Web3 Marketing

  • Enhanced Attribution: Better tracking across decentralized touchpoints
  • Privacy-First Analytics: Respecting user privacy while providing valuable insights
  • Transparent Metrics: Clear, verifiable data for marketing decisions
  • Ecosystem Integration: Seamless connection with Web3 infrastructure

Gratitude and Recognition

We thank our investors and supporters who believe in the transformative potential of Web3 and recognize the urgency of building its analytics layer now.

The future of the internet is decentralized, and we're building the tools that will power marketing in this new era. This investment round represents not just funding, but a vote of confidence in our vision and the critical role analytics will play in Web3's continued growth.
