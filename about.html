Our Mission

Providing the Intelligence Layer for the New Internet

The decentralized web is one of the most significant technological shifts of our generation, offering unparalleled transparency and user ownership. However, this new frontier operates in a state of data chaos.

At Cryptique, our mission is to bring clarity and intelligence to this ecosystem. We are building the foundational platform that transforms fragmented data into actionable growth strategies, empowering the next generation of Web3 builders to succeed on a global scale.

We're not just analyzing the new internet; we're giving you the tools to command your corner of it.

The Founding Team

Cryptique was founded by a team with a synergistic blend of deep technical expertise and proven Web3 growth leadership.

Parth Agarwal

Co-founder & CEO

Parth provides the strategic vision and go-to-market leadership for Cryptique. His experience is deeply rooted in the practical realities of scaling Web3 ventures. Previously, he was instrumental in scaling a crypto project from its inception to over $5 billion in cumulative transaction volume and built a vibrant Web3 community of over 5,000 members.

His time as an early-stage Deep Tech venture capital investor gives him a unique perspective on identifying market-defining technologies and building sustainable business models that resonate with institutional partners.

Akshit Varsani

Co-founder & CTO

Akshit brings proven entrepreneurial execution and deep expertise in scalable B2B technical infrastructure. As a second-time founder, Akshit built his tech services firm, AV IT Solutions, to over $500,000 in revenue by delivering mission-critical IT solutions.

He has led large-scale deployments of data pipelines and server infrastructure for major enterprise clients, including Denton Lawyers and ANZ Bank. His hands-on experience managing teams and delivering reliable technical infrastructure is foundational to Cryptique's architecture, security, and enterprise-grade service delivery.
